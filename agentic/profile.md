# Agentic Company Profile

## What We Do

Clay-powered GTM automation agency. We build signals-based outbound systems that detect buying intent, enrich prospects, and generate qualified pipeline. Campaigns go live in days, not weeks.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

- **Company type:** B2B companies with existing SDR teams
- **Revenue:** $2M-$20M ARR
- **Team size:** 2-5 SDRs
- **Core problem:** Sub-1% reply rates on cold outbound. SDRs stuck doing manual research instead of having conversations.
- **Buyer persona:** VP of Sales, Head of Growth, RevOps lead, or founder who owns pipeline
- **Disqualifiers:** Pre-revenue startups, companies without outbound motion, companies already working with a Clay agency

## Service Offerings

### 1. Signal Campaign Builds ($3,000-$4,000)
One-time build of a complete signal detection + enrichment + outreach workflow in Clay. Includes ICP definition, signal configuration, enrichment waterfalls, sequence copy, and CRM integration.

### 2. Monthly Campaign Management ($1,500-$6,000/month)
Ongoing management of 1-3 signal campaigns. Signal tuning, A/B testing, new ICP expansion, reply rate optimization, monthly performance reviews. Most clients run 2-3 campaigns at $4,000-6,000/month.

### 3. GTM Data Infrastructure (custom scoping)
Full CRM enrichment, account scoring pipelines, ICP definition. For teams that need the data layer before running campaigns.

## Signal Types We Monitor

1. **Funding rounds** - Detect new raises, enrich with headcount and decision-maker contacts
2. **Job changes** - Past champions switching companies, warm re-engagement
3. **Hiring signals** - Companies posting roles that indicate scaling pain
4. **Tech stack changes** - CRM migrations, new tool adoption during evaluation windows
5. **Website intelligence** - Scrape prospect websites, extract positioning and angles for truly personalized first messages (Agentic's unique differentiator)

## Pricing Framework

- Campaign builds: $3,000-$4,000 (one-time)
- Monthly management: $1,500-$6,000/month depending on number of campaigns
- No long-term contracts. 30-day cancellation on retainers.
- All Clay workflows and data stay in the client's workspace.

## Campaign Strategy Notes

### Research-First Outbound (Agentic's own acquisition strategy)

1. Batch scrape prospect URLs via Firecrawl (homepage + services/about page)
2. Claude classifies each company: what they sell, who they sell to, outbound angle
3. Generate personalized first messages for campaigns
4. For replies: trigger full research + lead list build
5. First test: 50 prospects targeting B2B service companies with SDR teams

### Key Positioning

- Lead with PAIN (manual research is slow and expensive), not TOOL (Clay)
- Website-level research as a signal is the differentiator. No other Clay agency does this.
- "Your SDRs research 15 minutes per prospect. We built a system that does it in 15 seconds."
- Don't claim to be big. Claim to be specific and effective.
